# QuickNote

## Blinn reflection model
$$
\begin{align*}
& C = I(\cos\theta\cdot K_d + K_s \cdot (\cos\phi)^\alpha)\\ 
& \cos\phi = n\cdot h \\
& h = normalize(\omega + v) \\
& \omega = \text{light direction} \\
& v = \text{camera direction}
\end{align*}
$$
